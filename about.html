CareClock is the first free comprehensive safety solution available. Our technology helps seniors to live more actively, independently, and safely.





Our CareClock solution allows family and professional caregivers to easily monitor all of their loved one's needs. Critical alerts are sent via phone, text, and email. The complementary caregiver app lets you manage settings remotely, get trended acitivity reports, and look in on your senior (when you choose the camera feature to be activate).

We provide peace of mind for both seniors and their families.



Our systems encourage families and caregivers to support their senior's desire for a more independent lifestyle. Our solution combines emergency notifications with non-invasive activity monitoring, so that if an emergency does occur, care providers can respond quickly and effectively to their loved one's needs.

We ensure that help is never more than a button press away.
